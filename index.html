Mental Health

Art therapy encourages creativity and self-expression; sensations that are generally suppressed and not experiences in the conventional workplace. It can, however, be used to counter those negative emotions that are experienced within the workplace: Art therapy is a portable form of therapy, and one that can be practiced alone or collectively.

Relationships

The usefulness of the art therapy modality in making visible the covert messages and hidden edicts from the family of origin will be illustrated. Through use of art materials couples are given an opportunity to solve problems in a manner which is unique and unfamiliar. The challenge of art tasks leads the couple to problem-solving and a creative approach in mastering a change in their relationship.

Abuse & Trauma

Using art to express emotion accesses both visually stored memory and body memory, as not only does it enable people to create images, but the use of art materials such as clay and paint can reconnect them to physical sensation. They may be cut off from their bodily senses, particularly in cases of physical and sexual abuse. Express yourself

You will gain an understanding of how much of a role emotion plays in artmaking as they create paintings based on feelings.

MATERIALS:

  • Acrylic Paint
  • Paint Brush
  • Something to paint on: canvas, hand, paper etc.
  • Background Music

WHAT TO DO:
Take a deep breath - and paint how you feel at that moment. It could be anything - a single dot on a paper or a Andy Warhol Painting. Once you feel like you are in the groove, put on some jazz music and paint to the emotion of the music.Play around with different lines - straight, jagged, squiggly, zig-zag, etc.
